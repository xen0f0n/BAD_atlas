#HackTheArctic 

The Bad Arctic Atlas


Disasters happening in the Arctic affect the regional climate, but also the ecosystems beyond the Arctic limits. Disasters can be either natural, such as wildfires, or man-made, such as oil spills, mining activity, overfishing etc.

The Bad Arctic Atlas is created as an interactive visualization medium to spread the disasterous activities which are happening in the Arctic, in real time.
